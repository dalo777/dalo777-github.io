Promise of Divinity Webtoon

I've been creating this as a self-directed project in Gatech's East Asian Media research group. It originally started as a black and white manga, and I've experienced the whole process of screenwriting to storyboarding to finally drawing. If you're interested, here is the whole first chapter. It's about a phoenix who loses her divinity and needs to retrieve it in the mortal world. I'm planning on eventually releasing it on Webtoon!

Rumor vs Reality Visual Novel

I was the art director for this visual novel that aims to help educate about misinformation. Here is our process book. We also have a demo for the good ending and the bad ending. I edited the demos with Da Vinci Resolve.

Your Mori advertising campaign remix

Our group of myself, Jacqueline Chambers, Lee Lee Jiang, and Soham Kar designed a remixed advertising campaign for Vtuber Mori Calliope's album Your Mori. The central part of our campaign is a 3D billboard set by the east exit of Shinjuku Station. I was mainly responsible for creating the 3D setting in Blender. Here is a clear picture of the building I modeled. Credit to chained_tan for Calli's 3D model!

Digidoor brand identity creation

I designed a brand identity using principles of visual and graphic design for the fictional company Digidoor, and am proud to announce that I'm a fictional CEO. The tools I used are Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. Here is the full brand identity report.
