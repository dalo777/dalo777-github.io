Promise of Divinity Webtoon

I've been creating this as a self-directed project in Gatech's East Asian Media research group. It originally started as a black and white manga, and I've experienced the whole process of screenwriting to storyboarding to finally drawing. If you're interested, here is the whole first chapter. It's about a pheonix who loses her divinity and needs to retrieve it in the mortal world. I'm planning on eventually releasing it on Webtoon!

Your Mori advertising campaign remix

Our group of myself, Jacqueline Chambers, Lee Lee Jiang, and Soham Kar designed a remixed advertising campaign for Vtuber Mori Calliope's album Your Mori. The central part of our campaign is a 3D billboard set by the east exit of Shinjuku Station. I was mainly responsible for creating the 3D setting in Blender. Credit to chained_tan for Calli's 3D model!

Digidoor brand identity creation

I designed a brand identity using principles of visual and graphic design for the fictional company Digidoor, and am proud to announce that I'm a fictional CEO. The tools I used are Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. Here is the full brand identity report.
